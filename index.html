Vince is a four-year old pitbull terrier/french bulldog mix who lives in St. Louis, MO. His stature may be on the small side, but his 30lbs of muscle projects a dog who's not to be messed with. He's sensitive, inquisitive, loyal and protective. He's the only one of his kind. He's Vince.

Athletics

Vince's favorite thing to do is play ball... especially at his Lovey and Papa's house. He's known to fall asleep clenching a tennis ball in his jaws.

Family

Vince was adopted from Stray Rescue, a no-kill shelter in St. Louis that rescues dogs from the streets. He has two moms and a brother, Tony.
